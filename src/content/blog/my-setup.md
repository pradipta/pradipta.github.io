Current work-from-home setup. Updated as gear changes.

## Computing

- **Work laptop:** 14" MacBook Pro (M4 Max)
- **Personal laptop:** 13" MacBook Air (M5)
- **Mac mini:** runs OpenClaw
- **Raspberry Pi:** hosts a few personal projects
- **Phone:** iPhone 12 Pro Max (primary, since Dec 2020); Pixel 8 (secondary)
- **Tablet:** iPad Air 4 (10.9", Wi‑Fi) for notes, meetings, and reading

## Desk and displays

- **Desk:** single-motor sit-stand from [Fonzel](https://store.fonzel.com)
- **Monitors:** two 32" [Acer EB321HQU](https://www.acer.com/us-en/monitors/essential/eb1/pdp/UM.JE1AA.C01)

## Input

- **Keyboards:**
  1. **Primary:** [Nuphy Air 75](https://nuphy.com/collections/keyboards/products/air75) — blue switches day to day; also have brown switches. [nSA Shine Through keycaps](https://nuphy.com/collections/keycaps/products/nsa-shine-through-abs-keycaps)
  2. [Royal Kludge RK84 Pro](https://rkgamingstore.com/products/rk84-pro-75-wireless-mechanical-keyboard) (brown switches)
  3. [Logitech MX Mechanical Mini](https://www.logitech.com/en-in/products/keyboards/mx-mechanical-mini.html)
  4. [Logitech MX Keys](https://www.logitech.com/en-in/products/keyboards/mx-keys-s.html)
  5. Apple Magic Keyboard (rarely)
- **Mouse:** [Logitech MX Master 3](https://www.logitech.com/en-in/products/mice/mx-master-3.910-005698.html) at the desk; [MX Anywhere](https://www.logitech.com/en-in/products/mice/mx-anywhere-3s.html) when traveling
- **Trackpad:** Apple Magic Trackpad, sometimes

## Audio and video

- **Earbuds:** [AirPods Pro (2nd gen)](https://apple.com/in/airpods-pro/) (primary)
- **Over-ear:** [Sony WH-1000XM4](https://www.sony.co.in/electronics/headband-headphones/wh-1000xm4)
- **Desk speakers:** Creative 2.1 soundbar
- **Webcam / mic:** [Lenovo 300 FHD Webcam](https://www.amazon.in/dp/B08KT89BWX/)
